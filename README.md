
# Customer Behavior Analysis Dashboard

# Project Overview

The Customer Behavior Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze customer purchasing patterns, subscription behavior, product preferences, and demographic trends. The dashboard helps businesses understand customer engagement, identify high-performing product categories, and make data-driven decisions to improve sales and customer satisfaction.

# Tools Used
* Power BI – Dashboard Development & Visualization

* Excel/CSV – Data Preparation

* DAX – KPI Calculations and Measures

* Power Query – Data Cleaning and Transformation

# Key KPIs
* Total Customers: 3.9K
* Average Purchase Amount: $59.76
* Average Review Rating: 3.75
* Subscription Adoption Rate
* Category-wise Revenue
* Category-wise Sales
* Age Group Revenue Analysis
# Dashboard Features
* Customer segmentation by Gender, Subscription Status, and Age Group
* Revenue and sales analysis across product categories
* Subscription vs Non-Subscription customer comparison
* Age-based purchasing behavior analysis
* Shipping preference analysis
* Interactive slicers for dynamic filtering and drill-down insights
# Business Insights
* Non-subscribed customers account for the majority of customers (73%).
* Clothing generates the highest revenue and sales among all categories.
* Young adults contribute the highest revenue and purchase volume.
* Customer ratings indicate opportunities for improving customer satisfaction and retention.
* Shipping preferences can be analyzed to optimize logistics and delivery strategies.
# Project Outcomes
* Identified key customer segments driving revenue.
* Analyzed purchasing trends across demographics and product categories.
* Improved understanding of subscription adoption patterns.
* Enabled data-driven recommendations for marketing, sales, and customer retention strategies.
